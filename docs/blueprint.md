# **App Name**: KABA DELIVERY

## Core Features:

- Admin Content Editor for Offers: Provide administrators with an intuitive interface to manage service offer names, detailed descriptions, pricing tiers, and upload corresponding WebP background sequences. This ensures the website's dynamic content can be easily updated and customized.
- AI-Powered Content Suggestions Tool: An integrated tool within the admin panel that utilizes generative AI to suggest engaging and varied marketing copy for service offer descriptions, helping administrators refine their messaging and appeal.
- Interactive Parallax Hero Section: Implement a full-screen hero section featuring scroll-tied animation of cinematic WebP image sequences. Scrolling down will advance the frames, and scrolling up will reverse them, dynamically reflecting the currently selected service offer.
- Service Offer Carousel Navigation: Develop the 'PREV' and 'NEXT' navigation system allowing users to smoothly cycle through up to five distinct service offers. This interaction dynamically updates the hero section's content and background sequence.
- Dynamic Theme Toggling: Enable website visitors to switch between light and dark viewing modes, and dynamically apply KABA's brand accent color throughout the interface to reflect premium branding.
- Structured Content Sections: Implement dedicated scrollable sections for 'About KABA', 'Why Choose KABA' (benefits), 'Detailed Pricing' with card-style layouts, and 'Physical Point of Sale', all accessible via a sticky top navigation bar.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Brand Color: A rich, deep KABA Red (#B31A33) symbolizing strength and urgency, without being overtly bright. This bold choice aligns with a leader in logistics, providing a premium and confident brand identity.
- Background Color (Light Mode Base): A very subtle, almost off-white (#F5EEEE) with a faint tint of the primary red. This high-brightness, low-saturation background provides a clean canvas that visually connects to the brand while offering optimal readability.
- Accent Color: A vibrant, refined magenta (#EB94D1), chosen from an analogous hue (approximately 30 degrees to the left of the primary red). Its contrast in brightness and saturation ensures key interactive elements and calls to action stand out, adding a touch of modernity and playfulness.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for its modern, techy, and bold appearance, perfect for large, uppercase offer titles. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for clear, neutral, and highly legible paragraph text, suitable for detailed service descriptions and longer content.
- Utilize clean, modern, and geometric line icons for general navigation and UI elements, reflecting efficiency and precision. For CTA images and marketing content, incorporate photo-realistic or high-fidelity 3D-rendered icons of smartphones and packages, designed with high contrast and reflective surfaces to convey a playful, premium, and efficient mood.
- Implement a full-bleed, responsive single-page design. The initial hero section commands attention with its dynamic background and layered content. Subsequent content sections will unfold vertically, adopting clear divisions and card-based presentations for information such as pricing, maintaining a structured yet fluid user experience.
- Focus on creating smooth, performance-optimized scroll-tied animations for the hero section's WebP sequences, providing an immersive, interactive narrative experience. Implement subtle and refined transitions for inter-section navigation and when cycling through service offers to uphold a premium and efficient aesthetic.
